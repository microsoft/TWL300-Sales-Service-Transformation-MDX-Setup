# TechExcel: Sales Service Transformation MDX Setup


## Course Objective:
Enable sales and service professionals to design, configure, and operate a unified Microsoft Dynamics 365 Sales and Customer Service environment that leverages modern AI-powered tools to transform customer engagement and operational efficiency.

## Learning objectives:
At the end of this workshop, you will be able to:
- Explain how Microsoft Dynamics 365 Sales, Customer Service, and Copilot work together in a unified architecture.
- Configure a Dynamics 365 Sales environment including lead management, opportunity tracking, and pipeline management.
- Set up Dynamics 365 Customer Service including case management, queues, and routing rules.
- Integrate Sales and Service processes to provide a seamless customer experience across departments.
- Enable and configure Microsoft Copilot for Sales and Copilot for Service to boost productivity.
- Use Power Platform tools to extend and automate Sales and Service workflows.

## Lab objectives:
By the end of this lab, you'll be able to:

* Provision and configure a Dynamics 365 Sales and Customer Service trial or sandbox environment.
* Set up the foundational sales configuration: accounts, contacts, leads, and opportunities.
* Configure Customer Service: cases, queues, service level agreements (SLAs), and routing.
* Integrate Sales and Service modules and verify cross-functional workflows.
* Enable Copilot features in Sales and Service and validate AI-assisted interactions.
* Clean up lab resources when the workshop is complete.

## Customer scenario:

Contoso Ltd. is modernizing its go-to-market motion and customer support operations by adopting Microsoft Dynamics 365. The sales team currently relies on spreadsheets and disconnected tools to manage their pipeline, while the service team uses a legacy ticketing system that is not integrated with sales data.

Contoso wants to deploy a unified Dynamics 365 environment where sales reps can manage accounts, contacts, leads, and opportunities from a single interface, and service agents can access full customer history when handling cases. The organization also wants to leverage Copilot to summarize customer interactions, draft emails, and suggest next-best actions.

In this lab, you play the role of a Contoso solutions architect. You will configure the Dynamics 365 Sales and Customer Service environment, establish integration between the two modules, enable Copilot capabilities, and validate the end-to-end sales and service transformation experience.
