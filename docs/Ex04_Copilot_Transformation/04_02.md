## Task 02: Enable and use Copilot in Customer Service

### Introduction

Microsoft Copilot for Dynamics 365 Customer Service helps agents resolve cases faster by summarizing cases, suggesting knowledge articles, and drafting responses. In this task, you will enable Copilot in the Customer Service Hub and use it to assist with a case.

### Description

In this task, you will enable Copilot features in the Dynamics 365 Customer Service app settings and use Copilot to assist with an open case.

### Success criteria

- Copilot is enabled in the Dynamics 365 Customer Service app.
- Copilot generates a summary for a case record.
- Copilot suggests a knowledge article relevant to the case.

### Key steps:

1. Enable Copilot in Customer Service settings.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. In Customer Service Hub, select the settings gear icon.
    1. Select **Customer Service admin center** or **Copilot settings**.
    1. Navigate to **Agent experience** > **Copilot for questions and emails**.
    1. Enable the **Copilot** toggle.
    1. Select **Save**.

    </details>

1. Use Copilot to assist with a case.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Navigate to **Cases** and open the case created in Exercise 02.
    1. Select the **Copilot** pane in the productivity panel on the right side.
    1. Review the AI-generated case summary.
    1. Select **Ask a question** and enter: `How do I reset portal access?`
    1. Review the suggested response and knowledge article recommendations.

    </details>
