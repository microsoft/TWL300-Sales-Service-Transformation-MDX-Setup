## Task 01: Enable and use Copilot in Sales

### Introduction

Microsoft Copilot for Dynamics 365 Sales helps sales reps work more efficiently by summarizing opportunities, drafting emails, and providing meeting preparation insights. In this task, you will enable Copilot in the Sales Hub and use it to summarize an opportunity.

### Description

In this task, you will enable Copilot features in the Dynamics 365 Sales app settings and use Copilot to summarize an opportunity record.

### Success criteria

- Copilot is enabled in the Dynamics 365 Sales app.
- Copilot generates a summary for an opportunity record.
- Copilot drafts an email for a sales follow-up.

### Key steps:

1. Enable Copilot in Sales settings.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. In Sales Hub, select the settings gear icon.
    1. Select **Sales Insights settings** or **Copilot settings**.
    1. Enable the **Copilot** toggle.
    1. Select **Save**.

    </details>

1. Use Copilot to summarize an opportunity.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Navigate to **Opportunities** and open the opportunity created in Exercise 01.
    1. Select the **Copilot** pane on the right side of the record.
    1. Review the AI-generated opportunity summary.
    1. Select **Draft an email** and review the suggested email content.

    </details>
