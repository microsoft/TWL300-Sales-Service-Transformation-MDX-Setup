## Task 01: Delete the Dynamics 365 lab environment

### Introduction

When you have completed all lab exercises, you can delete the Dynamics 365 environment to clean up resources from your tenant.

### Description

In this task, you will use the Power Platform admin center to delete the lab environment used during this workshop.

### Success criteria

- The Dynamics 365 lab environment is deleted from the tenant.

### Key steps:

1. Delete the environment.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Go to `https://admin.powerplatform.microsoft.com`.
    1. In the left navigation, select **Environments**.
    1. Select the checkbox next to your lab environment.
    1. Select **Delete** from the command bar.
    1. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

    {: .warning }
    > Deleting an environment is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure you have completed all exercises before proceeding.

    </details>
