# Exercise 03: Sales and Service Integration

## Exercise learning objectives
- Configure cross-functional views linking Sales and Service data.
- Enable service agents to view sales history on customer accounts.
- Enable sales reps to view open service cases on their accounts.
- Configure unified customer timeline across Sales and Service modules.

## Licensing and environment
- Dynamics 365 Sales and Customer Service licenses.
- System Administrator role in the Dynamics 365 environment.

## Roles and permissions
- Lab environment: System Administrator role.
- Real-world deployments: System Administrator or System Customizer role to configure forms, views, and relationships.

## Estimated time

Duration: **20–30 minutes**
