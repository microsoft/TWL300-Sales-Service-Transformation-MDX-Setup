## Task 01: Configure unified account views

### Introduction

Sales reps and service agents often need a complete picture of the customer. In this task, you will configure the Account form to display both Sales pipeline data and open service cases, providing a unified view of the customer relationship.

### Description

In this task, you will customize the Account form in the model-driven app to add a subgrid showing open cases, and verify that the unified timeline displays both sales and service activities.

### Success criteria

- The Account form displays a subgrid with open cases linked to the account.
- The unified activity timeline shows both Sales and Customer Service interactions.

### Key steps:

1. Customize the Account form.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Go to `https://make.powerapps.com` and select your environment.
    1. Select **Tables** > **Account** > **Forms**.
    1. Open the **Account** main form.
    1. Select **+ Component** and add a **Subgrid**.
    1. Configure the subgrid to show **Cases** related to the account.
    1. Select **Save and Publish**.

    </details>

1. Verify the unified timeline.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Navigate to **Sales Hub** > **Accounts** and open **Contoso Ltd.**
    1. Confirm the **Cases** subgrid shows the case created in Exercise 02.
    1. Confirm the **Timeline** shows both sales activities and service case activities.

    </details>
