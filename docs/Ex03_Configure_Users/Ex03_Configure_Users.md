# Exercise 03: Configure users and licenses

When cases are imported into the application, they are assigned to different users. In this exercise, you'll identify the user accounts in your environment, configure them, and add any that are missing.

## Estimated time

- **Hands-on:** 15–20 minutes

## Tasks

1. Identify the user accounts that you have in your environment
2. Ensure that your users are added to the correct Power Platform environment
