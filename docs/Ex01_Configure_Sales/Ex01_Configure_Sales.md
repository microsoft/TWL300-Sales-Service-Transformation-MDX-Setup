# Exercise 01: Configure Dynamics 365 Sales

## Exercise learning objectives
- Configure the foundational settings of Dynamics 365 Sales.
- Set up accounts, contacts, leads, and opportunities.
- Configure the sales pipeline and business process flows.
- Enable product catalog and pricing management.

## Licensing and environment
- Dynamics 365 Sales license (Professional or Enterprise).
- System Administrator or Sales Manager role in the Dynamics 365 environment.

## Roles and permissions
- Lab environment: System Administrator role.
- Real-world deployments: Sales Manager or System Administrator role to configure entities and business rules.

## Estimated time

Duration: **30–40 minutes**
