## Task 01: Configure sales settings and create accounts

### Introduction

This task walks you through setting up the core Sales configuration, including enabling required features and creating foundational data records such as accounts and contacts.

### Description

In this task, you will access the Dynamics 365 Sales app, configure basic sales settings, and create sample account and contact records.

### Success criteria

- Dynamics 365 Sales app is accessible and configured.
- At least one account and one contact record are created.
- Sales settings reflect the lab configuration requirements.

### Key steps:

1. Access the Dynamics 365 Sales app.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Go to `https://make.powerapps.com`.
    1. Select the correct environment from the environment picker.
    1. Select **Apps** and open **Sales Hub**.

    </details>

1. Configure sales settings.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. In the Sales Hub app, select the settings gear icon.
    1. Select **Advanced Settings**.
    1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Administration** > **System Settings**.
    1. Review and confirm the currency, time zone, and other regional settings.
    1. Select **OK** to save.

    </details>

1. Create an account record.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. In Sales Hub, select **Accounts** from the left navigation.
    1. Select **+ New**.
    1. Enter the following values:

        | Field | Value |
        |:------|:------|
        | Account Name | `Contoso Ltd.` |
        | Phone | `+1-555-0100` |
        | Website | `https://www.contoso.com` |

    1. Select **Save**.

    </details>
