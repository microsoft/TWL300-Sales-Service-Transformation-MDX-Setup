## Task 02: Create leads and opportunities

### Introduction

Managing the sales pipeline starts with capturing leads and converting them into opportunities. In this task, you will create a lead record, qualify it, and track the resulting opportunity through the sales pipeline.

### Description

In this task, you will create a lead, qualify the lead to generate an opportunity, and move the opportunity through the pipeline stages.

### Success criteria

- A lead record is created and qualified.
- An opportunity record is generated from the lead.
- The opportunity is progressing through the business process flow stages.

### Key steps:

1. Create a lead record.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. In Sales Hub, select **Leads** from the left navigation.
    1. Select **+ New**.
    1. Enter the following values:

        | Field | Value |
        |:------|:------|
        | First Name | `Alex` |
        | Last Name | `Johnson` |
        | Company | `Fabrikam Inc.` |
        | Topic | `New software licensing interest` |

    1. Select **Save**.

    </details>

1. Qualify the lead.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Open the lead record for **Alex Johnson**.
    1. On the command bar, select **Qualify**.
    1. Confirm that an account, contact, and opportunity are automatically created.

    </details>

1. Update the opportunity.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Navigate to **Opportunities** and open the newly created opportunity.
    1. Set the **Estimated Revenue** to `50000`.
    1. Set the **Est. Close Date** to a date 30 days from today.
    1. Select **Save**.

    </details>
