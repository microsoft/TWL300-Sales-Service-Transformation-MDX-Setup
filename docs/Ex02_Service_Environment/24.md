## Task 24: Verify your Chat widget has been deployed

-  If you do not already have it open in a different tab, open a new browser tab, and navigate to [https://make.powerapps.com](https://make.powerapps.com).

-  Navigate to Apps and locate the Customer Self-Service Portal that you created in the previous exercise.

-  Open the Customer **Self Service Portal**.

-  Verify that your chat widget is being displayed on the Portal.

> 
>   If you're still not seeing the chat widget in the portal, look at the working hours associated with the chat to verify that you're within the work hours defined.
