## Task 01: Provision and verify the Dynamics 365 environment

### Introduction

Before configuring Sales and Service, verify that your Dynamics 365 environment is provisioned and accessible. Confirm that required licenses are assigned and that you can sign in to the Power Platform admin center and Dynamics 365 apps.

### Description

In this task, you will access the Power Platform admin center, confirm the Dynamics 365 Sales and Customer Service apps are installed, and verify that your user account has the correct roles assigned.

### Success criteria

- Dynamics 365 Sales app is installed and accessible.
- Dynamics 365 Customer Service app is installed and accessible.
- Your user account has the System Administrator role in the environment.

### Key steps:

1. Sign in to the Power Platform admin center.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Open a browser and go to `https://admin.powerplatform.microsoft.com`.
    1. Sign in with your administrator credentials.
    1. In the left navigation, select **Environments**.
    1. Confirm your lab environment appears in the list and is in an **Active** state.

    </details>

1. Verify installed apps.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. Select your lab environment.
    1. Select **Resources** > **Dynamics 365 apps**.
    1. Confirm **Dynamics 365 Sales** and **Dynamics 365 Customer Service** appear with a status of **Installed**.

    </details>

1. Verify user roles.

    <details markdown="block">
    <summary>Expand here for detailed steps</summary>

    1. In the Power Platform admin center, select **Settings** > **Users + permissions** > **Users**.
    1. Search for your user account.
    1. Confirm the **System Administrator** role is assigned.

    </details>
